Coding Online Classes

Online classes have become a significant part of modern education, offering flexibility and convenience for students to learn from anywhere. With the rise of digital platforms, online coding classes have become increasingly popular, enabling individuals to develop programming skills at their own pace. These courses typically include interactive tutorials, coding challenges, and quizzes to help students practice and apply their knowledge. Platforms like Codecademy, Coursera, and Udemy provide a wide range of coding languages such as Python, JavaScript, and C++. These online classes are designed to cater to beginners as well as experienced developers looking to enhance their skills or learn new technologies.

Badminton

Badminton is a fast-paced and exciting sport that is played with a shuttlecock and rackets. It is usually played indoors, though it can also be played outdoors in calm weather conditions. The objective of the game is to hit the shuttlecock over the net, ensuring that it lands within the boundaries of the opponent's side of the court, while also preventing it from landing on your own side.Badminton is played on a rectangular court that is divided by a net. The court dimensions vary depending on whether the game is singles or doubles, with doubles courts being wider and singles courts being longer.

Cleaning room

Cleaning a room is an essential task that helps create a tidy and organized living space. It involves several steps, starting with picking up any clutter such as clothes, books, or dishes, and putting them in their proper places. Next, dusting surfaces like shelves, tables, and countertops removes any accumulated dirt and allergens. Sweeping or vacuuming the floor ensures that dust, crumbs, and debris are eliminated, followed by mopping if necessary to keep the floor spotless. Organizing belongings into drawers or storage bins helps keep the room neat, while wiping down windows and mirrors ensures a clean and fresh appearance

Cleaning room

Cleaning a room is an essential task that helps create a tidy and organized living space. It involves several steps, starting with picking up any clutter such as clothes, books, or dishes, and putting them in their proper places. Next, dusting surfaces like shelves, tables, and countertops removes any accumulated dirt and allergens. Sweeping or vacuuming the floor ensures that dust, crumbs, and debris are eliminated, followed by mopping if necessary to keep the floor spotless. Organizing belongings into drawers or storage bins helps keep the room neat, while wiping down windows and mirrors ensures a clean and fresh appearance
